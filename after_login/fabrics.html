Guide to Handloom Fabrics

Our handloom fabrics are sourced directly from artisan communities across India who use traditional weaving techniques passed down through generations. Each fabric tells a story of cultural heritage and exceptional craftsmanship.

  • Cotton: Breathable, comfortable and versatile - perfect for everyday wear in Indian climate
  • Silk: Luxurious, smooth and elegant - ideal for special occasions and festive wear
  • Linen: Lightweight, absorbent and cooling - excellent for summer clothing
  • Khadi: Hand-spun, sustainable fabric with a unique texture - the pride of Indian textiles
  • Jute: Eco-friendly, strong natural fiber - perfect for home décor and accessories
